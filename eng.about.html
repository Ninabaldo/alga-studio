HISTORY

Alga Studio was created in 2018 by Yumi Martí, Mía Ascanio and Andrea Álvarez graduates in Interior Design from the Bau University School in Barcelona.

MISION & VISI0N

Alga focuses on the creation of images, but also carries out interior design projects and artistic direction. The link that the study maintains with subtlety means that there is a concern for transmitting details, materiality, lighting and spatial composition.
